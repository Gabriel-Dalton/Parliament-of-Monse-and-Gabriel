DOMESTIC CONSTITUTION

We the People of this Household, in order to form a more perfect union, establish domestic tranquility, provide for the common defense against external threats (like in-laws), promote the general welfare, and secure the blessings of love and Netflix to ourselves and our posterity, do ordain and establish this Constitution for the Domestic Republic.
